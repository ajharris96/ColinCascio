Resume

My name is Colin Cascio and I am a graduate of Loyola University Chicago with a Bachelor of Arts in Ancient Greek as well as minors in Latin and English. I have also student-taught Latin and Ancient Greek courses under the guidance of my professors at St. Louis University & Loyola University Chicago and I had the privilege of working for St. Ignatius College Prep and St. Mary of the Angels where I taught Latin, Ancient Greek, and language arts courses. Working with language has become a strong passion of mine and I try to combine that with my love for creativity and writing.
